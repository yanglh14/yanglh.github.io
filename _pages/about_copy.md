I am currently a PhD student at University of Hong Kong under supervision of Dr. Pan Jia (HKU) and Dr. Song Chaoyang (Sustech). 
I received my B.S. degree from Tsinghua University (Beijing) in 2018. I worked with Dr. Huang Bidan (Tencent RoboticsX) during 2021.04~2022.07 in Tencent Rhino-Bird Elite Talent Program 

My research focuses on robotics perception and manipulation using learning-based methods. Specifically, I am interested in:
Developing a generic representation for world understanding through vision and tactile sensing;
Enhancing decision-making in unstructured environments with rich interactions.
